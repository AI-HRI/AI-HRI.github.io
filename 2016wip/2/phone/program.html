AAAI Fall Symposium Series 2016

November 17-19, Arlington, VA, USA

PRESENTATIONS

Full length and “lightning” oral presentations of accepted full and short papers. Authors of full length research presentations will be invited to participate in a panel discussion; presenters of lightning research talks will be invited to participate in a poster session.

KEYNOTE SPEAKERS

Keynote talks will give different perspectives on AI-HRI and showcase recent advances towards humans interacting with robots on everyday tasks. Moderated discussions and debates will allow participants to engage in collaborative public discussion on controversial topics and issues of interest to the AI-HRI community.

TOOL TALKS & HACK SESSION

Each tool talk will involve a presentation demonstrating the tool as well as an opportunity for attendees to use it during a Hack session on the last day of the symposium.

NETWORKING

A large part of this effort is to bring together a community of researchers, strengthen old connections, and build new ones. Ample time will be provided for networking and informal discussions.
